01 the day job

harnessing inferential gaps to defeat bounded rationality

co-founder of the simon institute for longterm governance. we help china and the west exchange on ai governance to ensure frontier r&d is understood across geopolitical fault-lines.

we started by strengthening international disaster risk governance before concluding that institutions that aren't ai-first will not have a future. now a team of 10 operating at the intersection of technical governance and intercultural exchange. for an example of what this kind of diplomatic entrepreneurship looks like, see how the history of cern can inform international ai r&d efforts. just that we’re doing it across countries that don’t (yet) believe they have shared goals.
